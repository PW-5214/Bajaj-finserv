SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2025, by and between:

COMPANY A, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of California ("Client")
and
SERVICE PROVIDER B, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of New York ("Provider")

1. SERVICES

1.1 Provider shall provide consulting services to Client as described in Exhibit A attached hereto.

1.2 Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner.

2. PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $10,000 for the Services.

2.2 Payment shall be due within 30 days of receipt of invoice.

2.3 Late payments shall bear interest at the rate of 1.5% per month.

3. TERMINATION

3.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice to the other party.

3.2 Client may terminate immediately if Provider breaches any material term of this Agreement.

3.3 Upon termination, Provider shall deliver all work product and materials to Client.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's confidential information.

4.2 Confidential information includes but is not limited to business plans, customer lists, and technical specifications.

4.3 The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of 5 years.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 All intellectual property created by Provider in connection with the Services shall be owned by Client.

5.2 Provider retains ownership of any pre-existing intellectual property used in providing the Services.

5.3 Provider grants Client a non-exclusive license to use Provider's pre-existing intellectual property.

6. LIABILITY LIMITS

6.1 Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the amount paid by Client in the 12 months preceding the claim.

6.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

6.3 The liability limits shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality or intellectual property obligations.

7. NON-COMPETE

7.1 During the term of this Agreement and for 2 years thereafter, Provider shall not provide similar services to Client's direct competitors.

7.2 The non-compete restriction applies to companies in the same industry as Client.

7.3 This restriction shall not apply if Client consents in writing.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration.

8.2 Arbitration shall be conducted in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

8.3 The arbitration shall take place in San Francisco, California.

9. FORCE MAJEURE

9.1 Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control.

9.2 Force majeure events include but are not limited to natural disasters, government actions, and labor strikes.

9.3 The affected party shall notify the other party within 5 days of the force majeure event.

10. GOVERNING LAW

10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

10.2 Any legal action shall be brought in the courts of San Francisco County, California.

10.3 The parties consent to the jurisdiction of such courts.

11. AMENDMENT

11.1 This Agreement may only be amended by a written agreement signed by both parties.

11.2 No oral modifications shall be binding on either party.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

12.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

12.2 This Agreement supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

12.3 If any provision is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in effect.
